01 / about

A bit about me

I'm a second-year B.Tech student in AI & Data Science at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune (CGPA: 8.84). My fascination with AI started after watching the AlphaGo documentary right after my 12th boards. That film made me realize machines could do things I never thought possible, and I immediately started learning Java and Python before college even began.


During my first year, I explored everything: web dev, game dev, AI, embedded systems. I volunteered for a state-level project exhibition, building their event website. I participated in my first hackathons, published my first research paper, and slowly found my focus in computer vision, deep learning, and real-world data problems.


Now in my second year, I lead AI/ML development at my college tech club, I have research papers in journals and conferences, and I'm actively seeking a summer research internship to learn hands-on from the best. I'm always building something, always curious, and always looking for the next challenge.
